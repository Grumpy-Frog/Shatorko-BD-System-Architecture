Shatorko BD — System Architecture

Hybrid last-mile early warning platform for Bangladesh. Combines centralized alert generation, LoRaWAN community relay nodes, localized Bangla audio, institution-assisted dissemination, and dynamic shelter guidance with acknowledgment tracking.
